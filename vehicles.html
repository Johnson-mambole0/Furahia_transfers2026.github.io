Road Freight

A modern fleet of freight vehicles across multiple weight classes. Full-load and part-load options, with dedicated runs for time-sensitive cargo across Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, and beyond.

Air Cargo Coordination

Strategic partnerships with regional and international carriers. We manage documentation, handling, and last-mile delivery for air freight shipments into and out of major East African hubs.

Secure & Sensitive Cargo

Specialized protocols for pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, electronics, valuables, and documents. Full chain-of-custody records, tamper-evident sealing, and security-cleared handlers.

Cold Chain & Perishables

Temperature-controlled vehicles and monitoring ensure the integrity of fresh produce, frozen goods, and cold-chain pharmaceuticals throughout the full transit journey.

Customs & Documentation

In-house customs clearance expertise and a dedicated documentation team. We navigate cross-border regulations seamlessly, minimizing delays and ensuring full compliance.

How It Works

Our streamlined cargo process ensures maximum transparency and minimum delays from the first call to final delivery.

Inquiry & Quote
