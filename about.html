Our Story

Why Furahia
Transfer Exists

Furahia Transfer was founded in 2005 to solve one straightforward problem: unreliable transport. Travelers were missing flights, businesses were losing clients, and hospitality guests were arriving frustrated. We saw a chance to do something different — to bring a genuine corporate standard to ground transport in East Africa.

What started as four vehicles on a single airport route has grown into a full-service transfer and logistics company serving 500+ clients across six countries. Our expansion was never driven by ambition alone — it was pulled by the trust of clients who wanted more of what we do.

Every service we offer today — airport transfers, executive taxi, cargo logistics, hospitality transport, and sightseeing — traces back to that original commitment: travelers and businesses deserve punctual, professional, and safe mobility delivered without excuses.
