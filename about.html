Our Story

Why We Started

Furahia Transfer was born from a real frustration. Our founder, James Mwangi, spent years watching travelers, businesses, and lodge guests suffer through unreliable, unprofessional, and unsafe ground transport across East Africa. Drivers who didn't show up. Vehicles that broke down. No communication. No accountability.

In 2005, he launched Furahia Transfer with four vehicles, two professional drivers, and one commitment: that every person who books with us arrives safely, on time, and feeling respected. Eighteen years later, that commitment hasn't changed — only the scale has.

Today we operate a fleet of 80+ vehicles, serve 500+ active clients across six countries, and handle everything from VIP airport pickups to cross-border cargo consignments. But the founding promise remains at the centre of everything we do.

Safety Commitment

Safety is at the
Centre of Everything

Transport is not just a service — it is a responsibility. Safety is not a feature we offer; it is the foundation every journey is built upon. Every vehicle, every driver, and every process at Furahia exists within a framework designed to protect the people and goods in our care.

Scheduled Vehicle Maintenance

Every vehicle follows a strict service schedule. Pre-trip inspections are mandatory. No vehicle leaves our yard in an unroadworthy condition.

Driver Safety Training

All drivers complete defensive driving training and passenger safety protocols. Certifications are renewed annually through our Furahia Driving Academy.

GPS Monitoring & Dispatch Oversight

Our 24/7 operations centre monitors every active vehicle in real time. Any incident, deviation, or emergency triggers an immediate response protocol.

5 Consecutive Years — Zero Critical Incidents

Recognised by SUMATRA for five consecutive years without a critical safety incident across our entire fleet. Safety excellence is not an aspiration — it is our record.
